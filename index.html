Introducing Lynn!

Lynn is the smarter way to wait in line. Stop wasting your time and start queueing virtually

Why Use Lynn?

Joining a queue

Lynn uses a virtual queue to hold your place in line. After joining a queue with Lynn, you can actively monitor your position in line. You can use our app to see how many people are before you and how long you have left to wait. You will get alerted as your turn approaches.

So step out of line.

Feel free to work, play, do whatever you want all while queueing virtually.

Managing a queue

Understand and control your queue! See how many users are in your line, see who those users are, and have complete control of your queue. Lynn will also be analyzing data from your queue and provide useful statistics about its performance.

The more often you use Lynn for queueing, the better our feedback will be!
